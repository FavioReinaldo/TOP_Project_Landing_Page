Some reasons to adopt a pet

Puppy mills are "factory style" breeding facilities that put profit above the welfare of dogs. Most animals raised in puppy mills are housed in shockingly poor conditions with improper medical care.

You are saving a life. Millions of healthy, adoptable pets die each year simply for lack of a home.

Having a pet animal can provide a sense of purpose and fulfillment as well as lessen feelings of loneliness and isolation in all age groups.

Pets have a way of putting a smile on your face and a spring in your step. Not only do animals give you unconditional love, but research confirms that pets provide us humans with psychological, emotional, and physical benefits.

“When you look into the eyes of an animal you’ve rescued, you can’t help but fall in love.”

— Paul Shaffer
